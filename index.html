What we do

A society built for those who govern

Pi Lambda Sigma (POLIS) is the University of Pennsylvania's premier nonpartisan pre-professional government society, open to all majors and schools and designed for anyone interested in a career in government or public service. We host speaker events, networking events, career workshops, and many fun social gatherings!

What sets POLIS apart

Nonpartisan

Λ

Selective
