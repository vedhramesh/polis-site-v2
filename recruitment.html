How It Works

The Process

POLIS uses a structured, semester-long application process designed to find students who are the right fit for our community.

1

Coffee Chats & Rush Events

At the start of the semester, interested students attend optional coffee chats and open recruitment events to meet current fellows and learn about POLIS.

2

Application

Submit a written application. We're looking for genuine interest in government and public service — not a perfect GPA or any specific major.

3

Policy Seminar

Selected applicants are invited to a seminar to engage with a pre-determined policy issue alongside current fellows and other candidates.

4

Initiation & NME

The process concludes with initiation and a New Member Education program to bring you fully into the POLIS community.

Eligibility

Who we're looking for

Any Penn student from any school or major who is seriously interested in a career in government, policy, or public service. That's the only hard requirement.

We particularly value intellectual curiosity, genuine commitment, and the ability to engage with perspectives across the political spectrum.
